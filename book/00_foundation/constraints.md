# Constraints

Hard constraints that shape the book. Every decision should be checked against this list.

## Length

- **Target word count**: _(e.g., 60,000–80,000 words)_
- **Chapter count**: _(e.g., 12–16 chapters)_
- **Chapter length**: _(e.g., 4,000–6,000 words each)_

## Structural Constraints

- _(e.g., The book must be readable in any order / must be read sequentially.)_
- _(e.g., Each chapter must be self-contained enough for excerpt publication.)_
- _(e.g., No chapter may depend on more than 2 prior chapters.)_

## Content Constraints

- _(e.g., No explicit technical jargon without definition.)_
- _(e.g., Every claim must have a cited source or be marked as opinion.)_
- _(e.g., No fictional examples — all case studies must be real.)_

## Legal / Sensitivity Constraints

- _(e.g., All names changed for anonymity.)_
- _(e.g., No reproduction of copyrighted material beyond fair use.)_
- _(e.g., Sensitivity review required for chapters dealing with [topic].)_

## Timeline Constraints

- _(e.g., Manuscript must be complete by [date].)_
- _(e.g., First 3 chapters needed for proposal by [date].)_

## Format Constraints

- _(e.g., Publisher requires Chicago Manual of Style.)_
- _(e.g., Ebook format must be reflowable — limit complex tables.)_
- _(e.g., Print format is 6×9 inches.)_
